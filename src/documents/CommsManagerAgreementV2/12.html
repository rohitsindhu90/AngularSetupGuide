11.
Term and termination
11.1
Subject to clause 11.2, this Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall
continue until either party notifies the other party of termination.
11.2
Unless agreed otherwise by Onecom, this Agreement will automatically terminate upon
termination (howsoever occurring) of any agreement between the Customer and Onecom
relating to mobile phone account management services.
11.3
For the avoidance of doubt, as the Services are provided free of charge, Onecom may suspend
or alter the Services, or terminate this Agreement, at any time.
11.4
On termination of this Agreement for any reason:
(a)
the Customer shall return and make no further use of the Software, Documentation
and any other items (and all copies of them) belonging to Onecom that were provided
pursuant to this Agreement;
(b)
Onecom may destroy or otherwise dispose of any of the Customer Data or Billing
Data in its possession; and
(c)
any rights, remedies, obligations or liabilities of the parties that have accrued up to
the date of termination, including the right to claim damages in respect of any breach
of this Agreement which existed at or before the date of termination shall not be
affected or prejudiced.

Variation
13.1
Onecom may change this Agreement (including changing the Services) where required: (a) to
