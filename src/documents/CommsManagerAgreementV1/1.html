the contract between Onecom and the Customer for the supply of the Services,

those employees, agents and independent contractors of the Customer

bill/invoice data relating to the Customer’s mobile
phone estate.

a day other than a Saturday, Sunday or public holiday in England when banks

for
the purpose of assisting with the management of customers’ mobile phone estates.

information that is proprietary or confidential and is either clearly

the contracting party wishing to receive the Services.

Services, and which may be amended from time to time by Onecom in its sole discretion.

the date upon which Onecom commences the provision of the Services to the
